# Sesame Miso Tuna Salad (For Tinned Tuna)

If you are looking to elevate a standard tin of tuna, you can make a delicious, high-protein salad or sandwich filling.

The Sauce: Whisk together 1 tbsp miso paste, 1 tbsp rice vinegar, 1/2 tbsp honey, and 1/2 tbsp sesame oil.

The Mix-in: Toss your drained tinned tuna with diced cucumber, celery, carrots, and green onions.

Finish: Pour the sauce over the tuna, mix well, and top generously with extra sesame seeds.
