# Peanutty chicken salad

Serves 1.

## For the dressing

1 tbsp soy sauce

1 tsp garlic + ginger paste (lazy hack, use fresh if you like)

1tsp sriracha

15g peanut buter

juice of 1/2 large lime, or 1 small

## For the salad
1 small carrot, julienned

approx. 10cm cucumber, cored and finely sliced

handful of mint leaves

handful of coriander leaves

75g frozen edamame, thawed

1/2 red chilli, sliced

1 cooked chicken brest, shredded

approx. 8g toasted peanuts, roughly chopped

1 tsp sesame seeds
