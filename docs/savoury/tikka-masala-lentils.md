# Tikka masala lentils

## Ingredients 
1 Tbsp oil
400g minced onions
25g minced garlic
2 blocks of taj crushed ginger
6 tsp tikka masala spice mix (M&S)
400g chopped tomatoes (1 tin)
3 cups dry brown lentils washed
8 cups water & veg broth mix
8 tsp puréed mango
Salt
Sugar

## Method
* Fry onions until golden. Add garlic for a 30s, then add ginger for 30s then add spices for another 30s.

* Add tomatoes and mash.

* Add a ingredients to a slow cooker and cook on high (hours) or on low (hours)
