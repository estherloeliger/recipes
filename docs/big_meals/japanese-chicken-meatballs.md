# Japanese chicken meatballs

!!! note
    test

## Ingredients
* 2 tbsp soy sauce
* 2 tbsp mirin
* 2 tbsp sake
* 1 tbsp sugar

Optional:
* Sesame seeds

## Instructions
* Season the ground chicken with salt and pepper

* Add chopped green onion, grated ginger, egg and potato starch. Mix well until combined.

* Shape into meatballs.

* Cook in a lightly oiled pan until browned on both sides.

Optionsl: cover the pan with a lid for a few minutes for extra juicy and fluffy tsukune.

* Pour in the sauce and simmer until glossy and coated.

* Sprinkle with sesame seeds and enjoy.
