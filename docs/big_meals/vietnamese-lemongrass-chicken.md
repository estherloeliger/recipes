# Vietnamese lemongrass chicken

## Ingredients per 2 servings (makes 2 meal preps)

1lb chicken thigh, skinless, boneless, raw

### Marinade
1 tbsp lemongrass

2 garlic cloves

1 tbsp shallot

1 tbsp soy sauce

1.5 tbsp oyster sauce

1/2 tbsp fish sauce (phy quoc fish sauce brand)

1/2 tbsp brown sugar

1 tbsp honey

salt and pepper to taste

1 second cooking spray oil

### Side

1 carrot
1 Persian cucumber
1 cup of grated cabbage
1.5 cup cooked rice

### Sauce

1/2 tbsp lime juice

2 tbsp sugar

1 tbsp fish sauce (phy quoc fish sauce brand)

2 garlic cloves

2 tbsp water

1 small red chili pepper (thai chili)

## Instructions

Thinly dice your shallot and thai chilis, slice your cucumbers into long strips, slice your carrots into small circles, and chop your cabbage into small pieces.

In a bowl, mix your lemongrass, minced garlic, shallots, oyster sauce, soy sauce, fish sauce, brown sugar, honey, and chicken thighs and set aside for 30min-2hours in a fridge.

In a pan on medium heat coated with spray oil, cook your chicken and season with salt and pepper to taste.

In a small bowl, mix together lime juice, sugar, fish sauce, minced garlic, water and thai chilis to make your sauce.

In a meal prep container, add half your cooked rice, half your carrots, half your cucumbers, half your cabbage, and half your lemongrass chicken and enjoy with half of the sauce!
