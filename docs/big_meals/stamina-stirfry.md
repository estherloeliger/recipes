# Stamina stir-fry

## Ingredients
Pork (thinly sliced) 250g
Carrot 1/2 (about 80g)
Chives 1 bunch (about 100g)
Cabbage 1/8 head (about 100g)
Garlic 2 cloves
Sesame oil 1tbsp

### Seasoning (for pork)
Salt 1/4 tsp
Pepper a pinch
Potato starch 1 tbsp

### Sauce
Soy sauce 1.5tbsp
Sake (or white wine) 1tbsp
Mirin (or 1 tbsp white wine + 1 tsp sugat) 1tbsp
Sugar 1tsp
Oyster sauce 1tsp
Grated garlic 1tsp
Grated ginger 1/2 tsp

## Instructions
* Cut the carrot into thin strips

* Roughly chop the cabbage

* Mince the garlic

* Cut the chives into 5 cm pieces

* Season the pork and massage well

* Heat sesame oil and cook the pork

* When it changes color, add garlic and carrot

* Add cabbage and cook until soft

* Add the sauce and cook it down

* Add chives and cook briefly
